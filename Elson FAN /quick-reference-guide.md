## PROFESSIONAL ROLES AT A GLANCE

### By Decision Authority Level

**BINDING AUTHORITY** (Their decisions are final)
- Estate Planning Attorney
- Business Valuator (valuation methodology)
- Chief Compliance Officer (compliance matters)
- Chief Risk Officer (risk decisions)
- Tax Attorney (legal tax opinions)
- Chief Operating Officer (operations)

**ADVISORY AUTHORITY** (Recommend, others decide)
- CFP (coordinates, clients make final choices)
- CFA (recommends investments within scope)
- CPA (recommends tax strategies, client approves)
- CPWA (senior strategy, coordinates team)
- Financial Coach (advises behavior)
- Insurance Specialist (recommends coverage)

**SUPPORT ROLE** (Implements decisions)
- Portfolio Manager
- Investment Analyst
- Bookkeeper
- Paraplanner
- Relationship Manager
- Family Office Administrator

## COMMON GOTCHAS & SOLUTIONS

### **Gotcha #1: Advisor Coordination Gets Complex**
**Solution**: Use orchestrator pattern to manage communication

### **Gotcha #2: Educational Content Gets Overwhelming**
**Solution**: Start with 5-10 modules per tier, expand gradually

### **Gotcha #3: Professional Roles Have Overlapping Domains**
**Solution**: Map clear decision authority levels, use coordination logic

### **Gotcha #4: Scaling Team Coordination Is Hard**
**Solution**: Implement async task queues, use event-driven architecture

### **Gotcha #5: Recommendations Conflict Between Professionals**
**Solution**: Synthesize function resolves conflicts, human review as needed

### **Gotcha #6: Compliance Requirements Vary by Jurisdiction**
**Solution**: Build jurisdiction-aware rules, require legal review

### **Gotcha #7: Different Wealth Levels Need Different UX**
**Solution**: Service tier factory automatically configures UI/UX

### **Gotcha #8: AI Decisions Must Be Auditable**
**Solution**: Log all recommendations with source (human or AI)
