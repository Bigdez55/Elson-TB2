### March 24, 2025: Micro-Investing Implementation âœ…

We have successfully implemented our micro-investing features for the Elson Wealth Trading Platform beta. This feature set includes:

1. **Fractional Share Trading**
   - Allows users to invest with as little as $0.01
   - Fully integrated with existing portfolio management system
   - Supports market orders for fractional amounts

2. **Round-Up Investing**
   - Automatically collects spare change from transactions
   - Configurable frequency (daily, weekly, or threshold-based)
   - Customizable multiplier (1x, 2x, 3x, etc.)
   - Detailed tracking and reporting

3. **Educational Integration**
   - New comprehensive "Micro-Investing Basics" educational module
   - Interactive explanations of fractional shares and dollar-cost averaging
   - Personalized recommendations based on user's risk profile

4. **User Experience Enhancements**
   - Streamlined micro-investment form
   - Round-up settings management
   - Direct integration with portfolio builder
   - Risk assessment for micro-investments

This implementation addresses our goal of making investing more accessible to younger users and those with limited funds. The educational component ensures users understand the principles behind micro-investing strategies.
