### **1. Stash**
**Markets Used**:
- **Stock Market**:  
  - Provides access to individual stocks listed on major exchanges like the NYSE (New York Stock Exchange) and NASDAQ.  
  - Supports fractional shares, allowing users to invest small amounts in expensive stocks.  

- **ETF Market**:  
  - Offers curated Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs) that track various industries, sectors, or indices (e.g., S&P 500 ETFs).  
  - Examples include Vanguard ETFs and BlackRock iShares ETFs.

- **Retirement Accounts**:  
  - Offers IRAs (Individual Retirement Accounts), including Traditional and Roth IRAs, for long-term tax-advantaged investing.  

**Additional Features**:
- Focus on beginner investors with educational tools.
- Automated investing with customizable portfolios.

---

### **2. Acorns**
**Markets Used**:
- **Stock Market (via ETFs)**:  
  - Invests users’ funds into diversified portfolios of ETFs that track stock indices like the S&P 500, Russell 2000, or MSCI World Index.  
  - Examples include Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF and BlackRock ETFs.

- **Bond Market (via Bond ETFs)**:  
  - Includes bond ETFs in portfolios to provide stability and reduce risk.  
  - Examples include U.S. Treasury Bonds and Corporate Bond ETFs.

**Additional Features**:
- Automated micro-investing by rounding up spare change from purchases.
- Pre-built portfolios tailored to risk tolerance (e.g., conservative, moderate, aggressive).

---

### **3. Robinhood**
**Markets Used**:
- **Stock Market**:  
  - Provides access to individual stocks listed on major exchanges like NYSE, NASDAQ, and AMEX.  
  - Supports fractional shares for small-scale investing.

- **ETF Market**:  
  - Offers a wide range of ETFs for diversified investing, including sector-specific ETFs (e.g., technology or healthcare).  

- **Options Market**:  
  - Allows users to trade options contracts for speculative or hedging purposes.  
  - Includes call and put options for stocks and ETFs.

- **Cryptocurrency Market**:  
  - Enables trading of popular cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Dogecoin (DOGE), Litecoin (LTC), and others.  

**Additional Features**:
- Commission-free trading across all asset classes.
- Margin trading for advanced users.
- Real-time market data integration.

---

### **4. Stockpile**
**Markets Used**:
- **Stock Market**:  
  - Provides access to individual stocks listed on major exchanges like NYSE and NASDAQ.  

- **ETF Market**:  
  - Offers sector-specific and diversified ETFs for long-term investing.

**Additional Features**:
- Specializes in fractional share investing.
- Unique gifting feature where users can buy gift cards redeemable for stock investments.
- Focus on educating younger investors and families.

---

### **5. Coinbase**
**Markets Used**:
- **Cryptocurrency Market**:  
  - Coinbase is a cryptocurrency exchange that supports buying, selling, trading, and holding digital assets such as Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Solana (SOL), Cardano (ADA), and thousands of other cryptocurrencies.  

- **DeFi Markets**:  
  - Supports decentralized finance protocols through staking services for assets like Ethereum (ETH) and Solana (SOL).  
  - Offers lending services where users can earn interest on their crypto holdings.

**Additional Features**:
- Advanced trading tools for professional traders via Coinbase Pro.
- Custody solutions for institutional investors.
- Wallet integrations for managing private keys securely.
